**Description** (≤4000):
```
Reading was never meant to be lonely.

Flipbook turns the books you're already reading into living communities. Every
page becomes a conversation with the people reading alongside you.

Start a reading community in seconds. Invite your friends, your group chat, your
book club. Pick a book, open it, and read together, at your own pace, but never

REACT ON THE PAGE
Drop a reaction or a comment right where it hit you. See what your friends felt

Watch your community move through a book together. Who's ahead, who just started,

A READER-FIRST READER

left off, on any device, even offline.

FOR THE PEOPLE WHO MAKE READING SOCIAL

Flipbook is built by readers who tried every app that promised to make reading
social, and walked away from all of them. This is the one we wished existed.

Start a community. Bring a friend. Read together.
```
